The LV-426 Incident

In 2122, the starship "Nostromo" was the first Weyland-Yutani vessel to encounter Xenomorph XX121.

Years of interaction with Xenomorph XX121 and extensive research were seized by the United Systems Armed Forces, hindering our progress.
